Date Size Increment Size?
For each backup entry, the increment size is the amount of data that changed in the subsequent backup. Said another way, the increment size is the amount of disk space that could be freed if the ability to restore to that backup date would be removed.
You would free ^totalIncrementSize$ if you removed the ability to restore from any prior backup.
